Combat Ruleset

A comprehensive guide to understanding Priority, Stagger, and Combat Outcomes.

Core Principle

To win a trade, your Stagger (S) must be greater than their Defense (D), while your Defense successfully holds against their Stagger.

Victory Example: Martial Art (S2, D1) vs Light Attack (S1, D0) = Winner: (Stagger beats Defense and Defense holds against Stagger).

If both players break through defense (Stagger > Defense for both), you get Both Staggered. In this state, generally whoever casts the ability first wins the trade; however, high Ping can cause variations.

$

The Four Outcomes

1. Victory (Win)

Your Stagger is higher than their Defense, AND their Stagger is lower than or equal to your Defense. You will cleanly break their attack and Stagger them.

Example: Your Martial Art (S2, D1) vs Their Light Attack (S1, D0). Your Stagger breaks their Defense, and your Defense successfully holds against their Stagger.
2. Defeat (Loss)

Their Stagger is higher than your Defense, AND your Stagger is lower than or equal to their Defense. They will break your attack and clearly Stagger you.

Example: Your Light Attack (S1, D0) vs Their Martial Art (S2, D1). Their Stagger breaks your Defense, and their Defense successfully holds against your Stagger.
3. Both Staggered (Clash)

Both players have a Stagger that successfully breaks the opponent's Defense. The winner goes to whichever attack hits and registers first. Due to varied animation speeds, a player who casts their ability first can sometimes lose to a player who casted second, due to a faster animation speed, allowing that player to Stagger first.

Example: Heavy Attack (S1, D0) vs Light Charged Skill (S1, D0). Both attacks possess a Stagger (1) capable of breaking the opposing Defense (0). Whoever directly lands the hit first will win the trade.
4. Both Defend (Draw)

Neither player has a Stagger high enough to break the opponent's Defense. Both players safely hit each other, but not Stagger.

Example: Special Skill (S0, D1) vs Special Skill (S0, D1). Neither Stagger (0) is high enough to punch through a Defense (1), causing both players to draw. (A Stagger of 1 vs a Defense of 1 also results in a draw, as Stagger MUST be higher in value.)

Important PvP Information

Inner Way and Attunement information that can cause priority differences.

Heavenquaker Spear (Martial Art)
2 Stagger
3 Defense
Wolfchaser Tier 3 Inner Way elevates the Martial Art Defense to 3 (Tenacity). This site uses the standardized 2 / 3 values for calculations.
Snowparting Blade (Special Skill)
4 Stagger
3 Defense
Initial hits are 2. The final Special Skill hit elevates to 4 (Super Armor) to break Tenacity. This site calculates based on the final hit.
Snowparting Blade (Heavy Varied Combo)
2 Stagger
3 Defense
This ability is granted by the Frost-Clad Night Tier 0 Inner Way and standardized at 3 (Tenacity). This site calculates based on this unlocked 2 / 3 configuration.
Vernal Umbrella (Special)
0 Stagger
1 Defense
Calculation Standard
Activating the Special executes an initial kick (Melee) at 2 Stagger, then summons the Turret at 0 Stagger. This site calculates based on the Ranged/Turret hit.
Fans & Umbrellas (Light)
0 Stagger
0 Defense
Melee attacks are 1 Stagger, while Ranged are 0. Light Attacks are 0 on initial hits and 1 on the final hit. The site calculates based on the Ranged (0) engaging standard.
Stormbreaker Spear (Special)
2 Stagger
4 Defense
Landing the Special Skill slam grants 2 seconds of Super Armor 4 via a Disc Attunement. This site calculates based on this buff configuration.
Soaring Spin (Perfect Dodge)
2 Stagger
3 Defense
Executing a Perfect Dodge elevates Soaring Spin to Tenacity 3. This site calculates based on this proc'd condition instead of the base Defense 1.
Golden Body (Synergy)
0 Stagger
1 Defense
Default activation provides Defense 1. Pairing with Shields (e.g. Thundercry) elevates it to Tenacity 3. This site calculates for standalone use for Qi Regen.
Serene Breeze (Attunement)
2 Stagger
4 Defense
Automatic cast provides Super Armor 4. Helmet Attunement then grants Tenacity 3 after the effect ends. Manual presses can be staggered by Divine Counter.
